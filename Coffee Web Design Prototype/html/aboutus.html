CEO & Founder

Edmond Dantes - Founder

Founded by Edmond Dante, a former front-end developer and designer, MR.COFFEE began as a passion project born from late nights, big dreams, and a deep love for coffee. Growing up in a small town where coffee was a daily ritual, Edmond combined her eye for design with her dedication to quality, roasting beans in her kitchen and selling to friends and local markets.

What started as a small side hustle has grown into a beloved brand known for its craft, warmth, and authenticity. Today, MR.COFFEE is more than just a coffee business—it’s Edmond’s way of connecting people through meaningful, slow-brewed moments.

Our Mission

Tradition meets innovation in every roast. Our beans are roasted with precision and care, blending timeless techniques with modern flair. We aim to elevate coffee culture by delivering consistently exceptional taste.

Our Story

2019 - Asheville, North Carolina

MR.COFFEE was founded in 2019 in the quaint town of Asheville, North Carolina, by Edmond Dantes, a former front-end developer and designer with a deep love for coffee and craftsmanship. What began as a late-night creative outlet—roasting small batches of beans in her kitchen—soon evolved into a passionate pursuit. Growing up in a town where coffee was more than a drink—it was a daily ritual—Edmond blended her keen design sensibility with an unwavering dedication to quality.

Selling her first bags of hand-roasted coffee to friends and local markets, Edmond quickly discovered that each cup had the power to spark connection and warmth. Word spread, and what started as a side hustle became a growing community built on authenticity and heart.

Today, MR.COFFEE is a beloved brand that stands for more than just great coffee. It’s a celebration of slow, meaningful moments and a mission to bring people together—one brew at a time.
