About Us

Edmond Dantes - Founder

Founded by Edmond Dante, a former front-end developer and designer, MR.COFFEE began as a passion project born from late nights, big dreams, and a deep love for coffee. Growing up in a small town where coffee was a daily ritual, Edmond combined her eye for design with her dedication to quality, roasting beans in her kitchen and selling to friends and local markets.

What started as a small side hustle has grown into a beloved brand known for its craft, warmth, and authenticity. Today, MR.COFFEE is more than just a coffee business—it’s Edmond’s way of connecting people through meaningful, slow-brewed moments.
